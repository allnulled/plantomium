[cms remove permission]
 [Usage:]
  · cms remove permission
     --name p
  · cms remove permission
     --name p
     --from-user u
  · cms remove permission
     --name p
     --from-group g
 [Info:]
  · Removes a permission from a user, a group or the auth system.
  · When used with:
   · --from-group: adds a new permission to a group of the auth system.
   · --from-user: adds a new permission to a user of the auth system.
